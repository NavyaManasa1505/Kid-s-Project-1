India lies in the continent of Asia. It is a big country divided into 28 states and 9 Uninon Territories. It is seventh largest country in the world. From west to east, it is 2933 kilometers long. From north to south, it is 3214 kilometers long

India shares its boundaries with many countries - Pakistan, Afganistan, Nepal, Bhutan, China, Bangladesh and Myanmar. Sri Lanka and Maldives are the nearest neighbours in the Indian Ocean

UNION TERRITORIES

There are some parts of India which are not included in 28 states. These are called Union Territories. There are nine union territories including the National Capital Territory of Delhi.
