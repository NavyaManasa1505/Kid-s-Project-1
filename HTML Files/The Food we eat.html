In India, the majority of people live in villages and farming is the main occupation in Indian villages. Farmers in India grow a variety of food grains, pulses, vegetables and fruits.

FOOD GRAINS

Foodgrains are one of the most important components of human life. We eat many types of food grains such as rice, wheat, maize, jowar, ragi and bajra.

  • People living in different parts of our country eat different food grains.
  • People generally eat wheat in northern India.
  • People eat jowar and bajra in western India.
  • Most people eat rice in eastern and southern India.

PULSES

Pulse are the edible seeds of plants. We eat different kinds of pulses. They are very good for health as they are rich in proteins.

VEGETABLES AND FRUITS

We get different vegetables and fruits in different seasons. vegetables such as green beans and pumpkin are available in summer, cabbage and peas are available in winter. Similarly fruits such as mangoes, cherries and watermelons are available in summer while grapes and oranges are available in winter. These days, however, many vegetables and fruits are available throughout the year.

OILSEEDS AND SPICES

In cooking, many types of oil and spices are used. We get oil from the seeds of mustard, coconut, linseed and groundnut. The different types of spices are grown in India. They make the food tasty and colourful.

SWEETS

Sweets, also called mithai are the significant elements of Indian food. Many kinds of sweets are made here. Mostly, people like Rasogulla, Payasum, Shrikhand, Phirni, Gajar Ka Halwa and Kaju Katli. Every state has its own special sweets. There are some sweets which are made on special occasions like festivals and weddings.
